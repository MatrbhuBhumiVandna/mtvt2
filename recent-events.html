Republic Day Celebration 2024

Our Republic Day celebration was a grand success with participation from over 500 community members. The event featured patriotic songs, cultural performances by Gurukul students, and a special lecture on the importance of the Indian Constitution. The highlight was the reenactment of historic moments from India's freedom struggle by our youth volunteers.

Gurukul Annual Day 2023

The Gurukul Annual Day showcased the remarkable progress of our students in both traditional Vedic education and modern subjects. Students demonstrated their proficiency in Sanskrit shlokas, Vedic mathematics, yoga, and classical arts. The event also featured an exhibition of projects blending ancient wisdom with modern technology, reflecting our unique educational approach.
