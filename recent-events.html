Our past activities and celebrations

Janta Raja Natak 2019

15th March 2019 Morbi, Gujarat

The Janta Raja Natak in 2019 was a grand success, attracting over 5,000 attendees across 7 days. The historical play depicted the life and struggles of Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj, inspiring patriotism and cultural pride among the audience. The event raised significant funds for the temple construction and helped spread awareness about our mission.

Annual Patriot's Day 2023

23rd March 2023 Matru Bhumi Vandana Trust

Our annual celebration to honor Indian patriots featured speeches by renowned historians, cultural performances, and an exhibition showcasing the freedom struggle. Over 1,200 students from local schools participated in various competitions including essay writing, poetry recitation, and patriotic song competitions.

Gurukul Inauguration Ceremony

12th January 2023 Trust Premises

The foundation stone for our modern Gurukul was laid in a grand ceremony attended by spiritual leaders, educators, and local dignitaries. The event included Vedic rituals, cultural programs by students, and presentations about the Gurukul curriculum that blends traditional knowledge with modern education.
