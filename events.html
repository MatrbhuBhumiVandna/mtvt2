Janta Raja Natak 2025

We are organizing Janta Raja Natak-2025 to raise funds for building the world's largest Bharat Mata Temple in Morbi, Gujarat.

Event Dates: 2nd May 2025 (8:00 PM) to 8th May 2025

Location: Matru Bhumi Vandana Trust Ground, Morbi

This historical play will showcase the glorious past of India and inspire patriotism among the youth. All proceeds will go towards the temple construction.
