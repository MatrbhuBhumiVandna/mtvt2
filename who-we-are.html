Who We Are

2017 - Foundation

Founded in 2017, Matru Bhumi Vandana Trust started with a dedicated team of nine visionaries, united by a common goal - to make Morbi proud by building the world's biggest Bharat Mata Temple and reviving the Gurukul/Vedic education system.

2019 - First Major Event

Organized Janta Raja Natak, a historical play, to raise donations for the temple construction.

Annual Program - March 23rd

Every year we conduct special programs to honor the sacrifices of Indian patriots on this date.

Present & Future

Continuing our mission to spread Sanatan Dharma, Indian culture, and nationalism through the Bharat Mata Temple and Gurukul education system.

Our Identity

Matru Bhumi Vandana Trust stands as a pillar of spirituality, nationalism, and social responsibility, working tirelessly to create a self-reliant and culturally strong India. Our organization represents the collective aspirations of patriots who believe in preserving India's ancient wisdom while embracing modernity, all while keeping our cultural identity intact.
